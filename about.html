About Us

Integrated Vessels Network Ltd is a rigistered limited liability company with a CAC No:123456. It is was established in October 2022. it is owned and managed by Mr Paul Asukwo Ubokulo, a Nigerian decent. The Company is into the oil and gas and it's major portfolio is creating a synergy between ship owners.

The company provides a seamless, timely and efficient platforms for ship owners to hire vessels, shipping equipments, machineries and ship stores for their contracts.
